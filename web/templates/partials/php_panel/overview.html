How this stack works

Main `caddy-docker-proxy` stays global. When you bind a domain to your site, the panel rewrites generated compose labels for the matching PHP-FPM service.

Your sites, domains, databases, and DB users stay owner-scoped in this view, while all sites still share the same `php_mysql` and `php_pma` services underneath.

site1.com -> php_fpm_74:9000
site2.com -> php_fpm_81:9000
site3.com -> php_fpm_82:9000

DB host: php_mysql:3306
